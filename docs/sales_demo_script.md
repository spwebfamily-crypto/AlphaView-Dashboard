# Sales Demo Script

## Goal

Present AlphaView Dashboard as a technical asset for equities research, simulation, and paper execution.

## Demo sequence

1. Open the dashboard overview.
2. Point out the runtime mode badge showing `PAPER` or `DEMO`.
3. Show the seeded summary cards, equity curve, and PnL curve.
4. Move to `Live Signals` and explain the probability-to-action mapping.
5. Move to `Backtests` and explain costs, slippage, and risk gates.
6. Move to `Models` and show that runs and metrics are persisted.
7. Move to `Trades` and `Positions` to show the paper broker workflow.
8. Move to `Logs` to show operational traceability.
9. End on `Settings` and explain that live trading is disabled by default.

## Key lines to use

- "This is a research and paper-trading platform for US equities."
- "The system is intentionally paper-first and demo-ready."
- "Backtests are treated as research artifacts, not promises of future returns."
- "The platform is structured so a buyer can extend data, models, execution, or dashboard layers independently."

## Do not say

- "The model always wins."
- "This is ready for managing external capital as-is."
- "These backtests prove future profitability."
